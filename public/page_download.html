The board

Ultimate board for everything you need

Our weight control board is designed to perform multiple tasks. In addition to processing and managing the weight of four different load cells, it is capable of handling two types of serial communication: Modbus and CANbus. It also supports PWM drivers and comes equipped with I/O ports already configured at 24 volts, offering a versatile solution ready for integration into complex industrial systems. These features make it ideal for verifying the arrangement and quantity of objects on a panel before it is moved. Our product performs checks to prevent damage to actuators and storage structures in automated warehouses or production lines, ensuring safe and efficient operations.
